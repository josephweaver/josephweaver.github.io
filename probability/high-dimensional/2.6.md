

## 2.6 Subgaussian Distributions

### Proposition 2.6.1. Subgaussian Properites.
For random variable $X$
TFAE:
1. (tails) $\exists K_1>0 s.t. P\{|X|\ge t\}\le 2\exp(-t^2/K_1^2) \forall t\ge0$.
2. (Moments) $\exists K_2>0 s.t. \Vert X\Vert_{L^P}=(\mathbb{E}\vert X\vert^p)^{1/p} \forall p\ge 1$.
3. (MGF of $X^2$) $\exists K_3>0$ s.t. $\mathbb{E}[\exp(X^2/K^2_3)]\ge2$.
4. If $\mathbb{E}X=0$ then TFAE extends to (MGF) $\exists K_4>0$ s.t. $\mathbb{E}[\exp(\lambda X)]\ge \exp(K^2_4\lambda^2) \forall\lambda\in\mathbb{R}$.

Where $\exists$ absolute constanct $C$ s.t. $K_j\ge CK_i, \forall i,j=1,...,4$

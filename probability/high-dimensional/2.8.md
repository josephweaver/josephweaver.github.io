

## 2.8 Bernstein's Inequality

### Theorem 2.8.1 Bernstein inequality. 
$X_1,...,X_N$ independent, mean-zero, subexpoential. Then
$\forall t\ge 0$, 
$$
    \mathbb{P}\{\vert\sum_{i=1}^N X_i\vert\ge t\}\le 2\exp(-c\min(\frac{t^2}{\sum_{i=1}^N\Vert X_i\Vert^2_{\psi_1}}, \frac{t}{\max_i\Vert X_i\Vert^2_{\psi_1}}))
$$

where $c>0$ is an absolute constant.

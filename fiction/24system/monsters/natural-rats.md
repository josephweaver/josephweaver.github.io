## Natural Creatures: Rats

Use these for vermin encounters, attrition scenes, and environmental pressure fights.

---

## Rat (Single)

**Type:** Natural Beast (Tiny)  
**Action Economy:** `HF`  
**Movement Rate:** `10`  
**Stamina:** `1`

### Hit Locations
- **Head:** 1
- **Torso (Core):** 1
- **Legs:** 2

### Skills
- **Melee:** `2d12` (Rank 0)
- **Athletics:** `d10 + d12 + 2` (Rank 1)

### Attacks
- **Bite (H):** Minor vermin bite.

### Damage Rule (Single Rat Bite)
- On normal hit: deal **1 stamina** damage (not a wound).
- On Partial Critical or if target is already Bleeding: deal **1 wound** instead.

### Partial Critical: Bite
- **Scurry and Snap:** Deal bite effect and immediately reposition 5 ft without provoking.

---

## Rat Swarm

**Type:** Natural Beast (Swarm)  
**Action Economy:** `HF`  
**Movement Rate:** `8`  
**Stamina:** `3`

### Hit Locations
- **Core:** 6

### Swarm Body
- Called shots do not apply to the swarm.
- All successful attacks against the swarm hit **Core**.
- The swarm cannot be grappled, pinned, or disarmed.

### Skills
- **Melee:** `d10 + d12 + 2` (Rank 1)
- **Athletics:** `2d10 + 4` (Rank 2)

### Attacks
- **Swarm Bite (H):** On hit, deal **1 stamina** damage.
- If target is Prone, Restrained, or unable to reposition, Swarm Bite deals **+1 stamina**.

### Partial Critical: Swarm Bite
- **Overrun:** Target takes normal swarm bite and gains **Bleeding** (or `+2 DC` on next action if already Bleeding).

### Behavior Notes
- Swarm pressures exposed targets, fallen targets, and isolated targets first.
- Fire, loud disturbance, and sudden area disruption can break swarm cohesion at Guide discretion.

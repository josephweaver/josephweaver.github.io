## Natural Creatures: Canines

Use these as baseline templates for wolves, feral dogs, and larger dire-hound variants.

---

## Canine (40-80 lb Dog/Wolf)

**Type:** Natural Beast  
**Action Economy:** `HF` (no `R`/`L`)  
**Movement Rate:** `12`  
**Stamina:** `1`

### Hit Locations
- **Head:** 2
- **Torso (Core):** 3
- **Legs:** 4 (1 per leg)

### Skills
- **Melee:** `d10 + d12 + 2` (Rank 1)
- **Athletics:** `2d10 + 4` (Rank 2)

### Attacks
- **Bite (H):** Treated as a one-handed weapon (1 wound baseline).

### Partial Critical: Bite
- **Latch:** Target becomes **Grabbed** by the jaw (head lock).  
  If target is already grabbed, the canine may drag the target 5 ft and target takes `+2 DC` on its next action (except Break Free).

---

## Dire Hound / Dire Wolf (Baskerville Hound)

**Type:** Natural Beast (Large)  
**Action Economy:** `HF` (no `R`/`L`)  
**Movement Rate:** `12`  
**Stamina:** `2`

### Hit Locations
- **Head:** 2
- **Torso (Core):** 4
- **Legs:** 6

### Skills
- **Melee:** `2d10 + 4` (Rank 2)  
  (one rank above standard canine)
- **Athletics:** `d8 + d10 + 6` (Rank 3)  
  (one rank above standard canine)

### Attacks
- **Mauling Bite (H):** Treated as a two-handed weapon (2 wounds baseline).

### Partial Critical: Mauling Bite
- **Crushing Latch:** Apply **Latch** as above. If the target is already grabbed, the dire hound may drag 5 ft and deal `+1` wound.

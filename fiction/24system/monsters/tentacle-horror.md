## Abyssal Tentacle Horror
*(Apex Lackey Controller)*

A mass of muscular tendrils surrounding a buried core-mind. It overwhelms space by action volume, not single-hit lethality.

---

## Abyssal Tentacle Horror - 24-System Stat Block

**Type:** Mythic Controller (Apex Lackey)  
**Threat Role:** Multi-Target Lockdown  
**Action Economy:** `RRRRLLLLHH`  
**Mobility:** Remaining body mass functions as one leg-equivalent movement action each turn.

### Core Action Limits
- Has 8 tentacles total.
- May make up to **4 tentacle attacks** per turn.
- Has 2 head slots (`HH`), allowing focus on up to **2 targets** each turn.
- Tentacle attacks can only be made against focused targets.
- If only one target is focused, all 4 attacks may be directed at that target.

### Hit Locations
- **R Tentacles (group):** 8 (2 per `R` slot)
- **L Tentacles (group):** 8 (2 per `L` slot)
- **Head-Mind Nodes (`HH`):** 4 (2 per head slot)
- **Core:** 12

### Armor
- **Rubbery Carapace:** Integrity 3

### Stamina
- **Total:** 5

### Skills
- **Melee (Tentacles):** `2d6 + 12` (Rank 6)
- **Athletics (Body Shift):** `d6 + d8 + 10` (Rank 5)
- **Perception/Threat Read:** `2d10 + 4` (Rank 2)

---

## Actions (No Saving Throws)

All effects use attack rolls or contested rolls against defender average DC where relevant.

### Multi-Lash
- **RLHF:** Any 1 tentacle slot (`R` or `L`) per attack
- **Check:** Melee vs target defense DC
- **Use:** Up to 4 times per turn total (across all tentacles)
- **On Success:** Deal 1 wound.
- **Partial Critical:** Apply **Latch** (target gains one grapple lock) or deal +1 stamina damage.

### Constrict Pull
- **RLHF:** 2 tentacle slots + 1 head slot on the same target
- **Check:** Melee (contested vs target Athletics or Melee average DC)
- **On Success:** Pull target one range step closer and apply one additional lock.
- **Partial Critical:** Pull target and make target **Disoriented** until end of next turn.

### Ground Thrash
- **RLHF:** 4 tentacle slots
- **Check:** One Melee roll; compare against each focused target's defense DC
- **On Success vs target:** Deal 1 stamina damage and target loses 5 ft of reposition this round.
- **Partial Critical vs target:** Also applies one lock.

### Body Surge
- **RLHF:** leg-equivalent body mass action
- **Check:** Athletics (contested when crossing guarded lanes)
- **On Success:** Reposition one range state and keep up to 2 existing locks.

---

## Traits

### Distributed Threat
- Losing one tentacle slot reduces max attacks by 1 (minimum 1), but does not disable the creature outright.

### Dual Focus Mind
- Chooses up to 2 focused targets at start of its turn.
- Changing focus mid-turn costs one tentacle attack.

### Anchor Flood
- A target with 2 or more active locks from this creature takes `+2 DC` on non-Break-Free actions.

---

## Encounter Use

- Best as a battlefield controller with minions or hazards.
- Counterplay is severing tentacle capacity, breaking line of control, and forcing focus splits.
- Designed to pressure multiple players without one-shotting them.

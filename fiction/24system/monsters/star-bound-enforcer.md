## Star-Bound Enforcer
*(Apex Lackey Brute)*

An engineered war-servitor of higher powers: heavy, purposeful, and built to break lines.

---

**Type:** Mythic Brute (Apex Lackey)  
**Threat Role:** Breaker / Pressure Anchor  
**Action Economy:** `LRHF`  
**Movement Rate:** `5`

### Hit Locations
- **R (Right Arm):** 4
- **L (Left Arm):** 4
- **H (Head):** 3
- **F (Legs):** 6
- **Core (Torso):** 10

### Armor
- **Heavy Plating:** Integrity 4

### Stamina
- **Total:** 4

### Fear
- Immune to normal fear escalation unless driven by specific occult banes.

### Skills
- **Melee:** `d6 + d8 + 10` (Rank 5)
- **Athletics:** `2d6 + 12` (Rank 6)
- **Awareness/Lore equivalent:** `2d10 + 4` (Rank 2)

---

## Actions (No Saving Throws)

All effects below resolve with contested checks or normal attack resolution.  
No target saving throws are used.

### Siege Strike
- **RLHF:** `LRH`
- **Check:** Melee vs target defense DC
- **On Success:** Deal 3 wounds and shatter light cover in the struck lane.
- **Partial Critical:** Deal +1 wound or force target off-Guard.

### Trample Line
- **RLHF:** `F`
- **Check:** One Athletics roll by Enforcer.
- **Resolution:** Compare that single roll against each affected target's Athletics average DC (rounded up).
- **On Success vs target:** Target is knocked **Prone** and displaced 5 ft.
- **On Miss vs target:** Target keeps footing and may choose to yield 5 ft or remain engaged.
- **Partial Critical:** Targets the Enforcer beats by 4+ also lose one reaction until next turn.

### Crush and Pin
- **RLHF:** `LRF`
- **Check:** Melee (contested vs target Melee or Athletics average DC)
- **On Success:** Target gains **Pinned** until it breaks free.
- **Partial Critical:** Target is pinned and takes 1 additional stamina damage.

### Relentless Advance
- **RLHF:** `F`
- **Check:** Athletics (contested when entering guarded lanes)
- **On Success:** Advance one range state and ignore one non-magical forced-move effect this beat.

---

## Traits

### Unstoppable Mass
- First forced movement against the Enforcer each round is ignored.

### Siege Presence
- Enemies in adjacent lanes gain `+1 DC` on movement actions while in direct line of threat.

### Objective Pressure
- Whenever the Enforcer inflicts a Major Complication, advance the scene objective clock by one step.

---

## Defeat and Encounter Use

- This creature is intended as a high-threat apex servant, not a routine fight.
- Best used with objective-based scenes (delay, seal, extract, collapse path), not pure stand-and-trade.

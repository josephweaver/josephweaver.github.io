# President Rock

In a strange political experiment, an independent candidate campaigns on a radical promise:

If a five-pound rock could handle the situation by doing nothing, I will do the same.

After winning the election, the rock remains on his desk.

Over time, the nation begins reorganizing itself around the one thing the rock never does:

react.

The result is a political system where:

crises de-escalate

institutions begin solving problems themselves

people project their beliefs onto the rock

The rock never speaks.

The rock never moves.

Yet somehow it becomes the most stable presidency in modern history.
